Islamic Compliance Checker

This page is dedicated to checking compliance with Islamic guidelines for various activities and financial transactions.

Guidelines

  • No interest (riba) in financial transactions.
  • Investments must be made in halal (permissible) business activities.
  • Avoid businesses that involve gambling (maysir) or unethical activities.
  • Ensure that the contracts are clear and transparent to all parties involved.
  • Charitable giving (zakat) is encouraged and should be considered in financial planning.
